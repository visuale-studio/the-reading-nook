HOLDING UP THE UNIVERSE

rom com · personal thoughts

i absolutely loved this book — it was sweet in the most genuine and unforced way, the kind of sweetness that does not feel manufactured or convenient but rather like something that grew naturally out of two people choosing each other over and over again despite every reason not to. what i found most compelling was the relationship between libby and jack and the way it developed so gradually and so honestly. the trust they built between them did not come easily or quickly — it was earned through vulnerability and patience and a willingness to be truly known by another person, and watching that unfold had me completely swooning in the best possible way. libby in particular is such a brilliant character — bold, funny, fiercely self possessed and utterly unapologetic about who she is — and the way jack slowly comes to see her, really see her, for everything she is rather than everything the world has projected onto her, is genuinely moving. jennifer niven writes their bond with such care and emotional intelligence that by the time the story reaches its resolution you feel as though you have grown alongside both of them. a truly lovely book that left me with a full heart.
