THIS SONG IS NOT FOR YOU

rom com · review

this song is not for you is a sensitive, quietly observed story about the fragile and complicated nature of friendship when romantic feelings enter the picture. at its heart are sam and ramona, two best friends whose bond has been the defining relationship of both their lives — steady, safe and entirely their own. that equilibrium is gently but irreversibly disrupted when their band welcomes a new member, tom, whose arrival sets off a chain of emotions neither of them was prepared for. what follows is a new age coming of age story that attempts to explore loyalty, longing and the painful question of what happens to a friendship when both people want the same thing. the book handles its subject matter with a genuine sensitivity and emotional awareness, and there are moments of real tenderness buried within it that feel authentic and carefully observed. it is a book that clearly has its heart in the right place, and for readers who connect with its particular emotional frequency, it will likely resonate quite deeply.
