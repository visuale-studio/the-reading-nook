THE NATURALS

personal thoughts

this is personally my favourite series in this entire category, and it is not even close. cassie and dean are such a beautifully written couple — the slow burn between them feels earned in a way that romance subplots rarely do in mystery series, and every moment between them genuinely made me smile. while i did find lia and michael a little grating at times, i can appreciate that their personalities served a purpose within the group dynamic, and their own storylines did grow on me as the series progressed. what kept me completely hooked throughout every single book was the writing of the plot itself — so wonderfully constructed that i never once lost interest or felt the urge to skim. perhaps the most uniquely gripping element of the series is the "you" chapters, written from the perspective of the antagonist. they genuinely spooked me in the best possible way. there is something deeply unsettling yet completely enthralling about being placed inside the mind of someone dangerous — it added a layer of dread and psychological tension that elevated the entire reading experience and made every mystery feel that much more urgent and personal.
