BEFORE THE COFFEE GETS COLD

rom com · review

before the coffee gets cold is a warm, nostalgic series unlike anything else out there. each story takes you gently by the hand and walks you through time, letting you live life through the eyes of entirely different people — their regrets, their longing, their love, and the moments they wished they could return to. it is the very definition of a comfort series; cozy and sentimental in a way that wraps around you like a blanket on a rainy afternoon. what makes it truly stand out is the time travel concept — an unusual and seemingly unlikely addition to a series with no fixed overarching plot, and yet it works beautifully. rather than feeling gimmicky or forced, the time travel element gives the stories a quiet, melancholic magic that elevates every single human moment within them. it makes you think about your own life, your own memories, and the people you would go back to see if you only had the time it takes for a cup of coffee to go cold. unconventional, unhurried, and utterly unforgettable.
