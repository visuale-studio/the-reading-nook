ONE OF US IS LYING

review

one of us is lying revolves around five students who walk into detention at the same time, but only four make it out alive. when simon, the creator of the school’s infamous gossip app, suddenly dies during detention in what is ruled a murder, the other four students immediately become the prime suspects. each of them has something to hide, and as the investigation unfolds, long-buried secrets begin to surface, putting friendships, reputations, and lives at risk. one of us is next takes place about a year later at bayview high. when a mysterious game of truth or dare begins circulating through the school, what starts as a harmless prank quickly turns dangerous. as students are forced to choose between revealing dangerous truths or completing risky dares, the game spirals out of control, and three new students find themselves caught in the middle of another gripping mystery. one of us is back brings together familiar faces from both previous books when another deadly mystery shakes bayview. after a series of threatening messages begins targeting former bayview students, old secrets resurface and past events come back to haunt them. as danger closes in once again, the group must work together to uncover the truth before history repeats itself. together, the trilogy delivers suspenseful, character-driven mysteries packed with secrets, twists, and unexpected revelations.
