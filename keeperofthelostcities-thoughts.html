KEEPER OF THE LOST CITIES

personal thoughts

i love this series! it is gripping, intriguing and every ending of a book leaves me wanting more. the slow burn between sophie and keefe (and at some point fitz 😭) was such a good subplot, and i loved how there were so many side plots that were essential to the main plot in each book, but never overpowers the point being conveyed. however, there are some extremely lengthy chapters in each that did seem like they didn't need to be part of the book, and that contributed to the vast length of each book.
