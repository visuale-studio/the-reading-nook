THIS SONG IS NOT FOR YOU

rom com · personal thoughts

this one was unfortunately not for me — pun very much intended. while the opening chapters were interesting enough to keep me engaged and curious about where the story was heading, the momentum did not last. the plot grew increasingly difficult to follow as it progressed, and what had initially felt like a nuanced and layered story gradually became muddled in a way that made it hard to stay invested. and then, inevitably, ramona fell in love with tom. i wish i could say i did not see it coming from approximately the second chapter — but i absolutely did. the romantic turn felt predictable in a way that drained some of the energy out of what had been a more interesting dynamic between the three characters, and once it happened, the story lost me fairly completely. it is not a bad book by any means — it is clearly written with genuine care and emotional intention — but it did not quite deliver on the promise of its premise, and the predictability of the central romance made it difficult to reconnect with once my attention had wandered. sometimes a book just is not for you, and this was one of those times.
