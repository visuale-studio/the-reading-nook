FIVE SURVIVE

mystery / crime · review

five survive follows a group of six friends who set off on an rv road trip for spring break, expecting nothing more than a week of fun and freedom. their plans take a terrifying turn when their vehicle is intentionally disabled in the middle of nowhere, leaving them completely stranded with no signal and no way to call for help. they soon discover that a sniper is watching them from the darkness outside and has only one demand — someone inside the rv is hiding a secret that is deeply significant to him, and until that truth is dragged into the light, nobody is leaving alive. as the hours crawl by, panic and paranoia spread through the group like wildfire. friendships fracture under the pressure, hidden lies begin to surface, and the question of who can actually be trusted becomes as dangerous as the threat outside. with time running out and danger growing closer, every character becomes a suspect and every secret becomes a potential death sentence. holly jackson delivers a relentlessly tense, claustrophobic thriller that keeps you locked in that rv right alongside the characters, desperate for answers and terrified of what the truth might cost.
