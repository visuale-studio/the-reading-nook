INHERITANCE GAMES

personal thoughts

the inheritance games is a very well written book trilogy. following quite a common riches-to-rags story, it is written in such a way that is anything but ordinary. each riddle leaves you questioning your mental ability and you cannot guess what happens next! an amazingly written book. however, i felt that the introduction of numerous new characters complicated the storyline in the second and third books, and made it quite murky to understand.
