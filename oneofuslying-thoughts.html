ONE OF US IS LYING

personal thoughts

i really enjoyed the one of us is lying series as a whole, and i think each book brought something different to the table. one of us is lying had the strongest character development, and although i found the main twist a little predictable, the suspense and relationships between the characters kept me invested throughout. one of us is next introduced an interesting truth or dare concept that made the mystery feel fresh, and i really enjoyed the new cast, even if i preferred the first book overall. one of us is back was a satisfying conclusion to the trilogy, especially because it brought together characters from the previous books and tied everything together nicely. while i felt some of the twists weren’t as shocking as i expected, i still found the mystery engaging, and it was exciting to return to bayview one last time. overall, i’d definitely recommend this series to anyone who enjoys y/a thrillers with memorable characters, interconnected stories, and mysteries that keep you guessing until the end.
